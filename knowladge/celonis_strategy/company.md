## What Celonis Does

Celonis is the global leader in Process Intelligence. The platform ingests event log data from enterprise systems (SAP, Salesforce, Oracle, ServiceNow, and hundreds more), reconstructs how business processes actually run (not how they are assumed to run) and surfaces AI-driven recommendations to fix, automate, or orchestrate them.

**Core value proposition:** Give enterprise AI the operational context it needs to deliver real ROI, by creating a living digital twin of how the business operates.

---

## Platform Architecture

The Celonis Process Intelligence Platform is built on three layers:

| Layer | What it does |
|-------|-------------|
| **Data Core** | High-performance extraction, transformation, and storage of event log data from any enterprise system at scale. Including high performance PQL engine for millisecond latencies over billion of records. |
| **Process Intelligence Graph** | Semantic layer. Combines system data with business context (rules, KPIs, benchmarks, ML models) to create a system-agnostic digital twin of operations |
| **Build Experience** | Capabilities to analyze processes, design improvements, and operate AI-driven interventions and automations |

Celonis does **not replace** source systems. It sits as an intelligence and orchestration layer on top of them.

## Strategic Priorities (2026)

1. **Process Intelligence as the AI context layer** — Enterprise AI only delivers ROI when it understands how the business actually operates. Celonis's Process Intelligence Graph provides that operational context. This is the company's primary strategic narrative.

2. **From visibility to action to orchestration** — The platform has matured beyond process *mining* (seeing what's broken) toward process *orchestration* (autonomously fixing it). Features and messaging should reflect this progression.

3. **Agentic AI and multi-agent readiness** — 90%+ of enterprises are exploring agentic AI. Celonis is positioning the Process Intelligence Graph as the shared operational context layer for multi-agent systems. New features should consider how they enable or interact with AI agents.

4. **"Free the Process" / Open Ecosystem** — Celonis champions vendor-agnostic process intelligence. The platform must work across any source system combination, and avoid locking customers into specific tech stacks.

5. **Closing the operational readiness gap** — 76% of organizations say their current processes hamper AI implementation. Celonis targets this gap directly: features that reduce implementation friction, accelerate time-to-value, and lower the expertise barrier are high priority.

## Business Model

- **Enterprise SaaS** — annual contracts, typically multi-year
- **Land and expand** — initial deployment on one process (e.g. AP) or one business unit, then expansion across process scope, geographies, and departments
- **Value-based selling** — deals are sized against measurable business outcomes (working capital improvement, cost savings, cycle time reduction), not user seats
- **Partner ecosystem** — 250+ partners; many deployments are partner-led implementations
