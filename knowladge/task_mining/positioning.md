## 2. Problem statement

Organizations treat **AI-driven operations and automation** as strategic imperatives, yet many fail to show **measurable value** because work is **fragmented across systems** and **human steps are invisible** (see source note [1] in original materials).

**What process mining already does:** It creates a **living representation** of how processes run **across the enterprise and systems of record**.

**The gap:** A large share of work still happens **between** major applications—in **spreadsheets, productivity tools, and desktop software** where tasks actually execute. **Human-driven steps** stay invisible, so the operational picture is **incomplete**. Without **secure, scaled** capture of manual tasks, companies struggle to find **root causes**, **prioritize improvements**, and—critically—**ground AI agents** with reliable context, blocking the move to **truly autonomous workflows**.

---

## 3. Three key challenges (bullets)

1. **Invisible task silos**  
   Process mining shows **systems of record**; **manual work between systems** stays hidden. That creates **blind spots** and prevents a full picture of **how work is really done**.

2. **Unstructured desktop activity**  
   At enterprise scale, desktop data is a **chaotic stream** of **millions** of granular actions. Volume and noise make it hard to separate **process-relevant work** from **noise**, to **cluster** into business activities, and to see **which habits** slow the process.

3. **Fragmented AI context**  
   Agents and automations often underperform when they lack **operational memory** of real task execution, driving **unreliable outcomes** and **low trust** in autonomous systems.

## 7. Key features (with descriptions)

### 7.1 Enterprise-grade data capturing

- **Scalable Task Mining clients**  
  Secure capture of manual activity on **user desktops** (Windows; cloud desktop / VDI and additional OS on roadmap) at **enterprise scale**. Primary data source is **UI metadata and DOM elements** (click coordinates, active window, URLs, page titles) — screenshots are a supported but **supplementary** signal, not the primary extraction mechanism.

- **Automated data masking**  
  Sustains compliance in **regulated** environments (e.g. **GDPR, SOC, ISO**) via **PII masking** and **privacy** controls. Processing happens **locally** on the client before ingestion.

- **Hybrid data gateway**  
  Supports **localized** data governance and **auditability** by allowing **on-premises** storage **before** ingestion to Celonis.

### 7.2 Process-aware data intelligence

- **Worker productivity insights (Work Perspectivity app)**  
  Entry point for most customers: **out-of-the-box** dashboards covering workforce productivity, application usage, and time distribution. Delivers immediate time-to-value without any customer build effort.

- **Task discovery**  
  **LLM-powered clustering** of raw clickstream noise into **meaningful, named business tasks** (e.g. "process invoice," "reconcile discrepancy"), mapped to **business context** — objects, applications, process steps — and aligned with the **Process Intelligence Graph**.

- **Platform-native interoperability — Analyze → Design → Operate**  
  **Connects** task insights to the full **Celonis Build Experience**: surface inefficiencies (**Analyze**), model target-state with full current-state visibility (**Design**), then deploy improvements via orchestration, RPA, or **Agentic AI** grounded in operational reality (**Operate**).

## 12. Do not position (guardrails)

Use these as **hard negations** in messaging and agent answers:

- **Not** a **standalone** task-mining play whose **primary** goal is **only** to **automate manual tasks** (e.g. with **RPA**). Task Mining is **embedded in the Celonis Platform** and serves **digital twin** and **Agentic AI** outcomes—not **point** RPA harvesting alone.
- **Not** a **surveillance or monitoring** platform. Privacy and user trust are non-negotiable constraints. Emphasize opt-in governance, configurable capture scope, and automated masking.
- **Not** a **replacement for process mining** — it is an additive, complementary data layer. Process mining handles system-of-record event logs; Task Mining adds the desktop/human layer on top.
- **Do not** overclaim beyond **approved** assets; keep **Siemens** and **success links** in sync with **GTM** when WIP completes.
