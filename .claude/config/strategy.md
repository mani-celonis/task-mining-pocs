## 1. FY27 Strategic Vision (L1)

Our ambition is to make the Celonis Platform the **cornerstone of our customers' evolution to becoming AI-driven and composable**. The vision is anchored in five pillars:

*   **Enterprise Quality:** Moving beyond functionality to provide a platform with 99.9% availability, security, and performance suitable for mission-critical workloads.
*   **Complete Digital Twin (DT):** Building the most complete DT by expanding the **Process Intelligence Graph (PI Graph)** to include task mining, unstructured data, and cross-company network data.
*   **Unify Analyze – Design – Operate (ADO):** Connecting the practices of analysis, design, and operation into a single, unified lifecycle.
*   **Scale Operational Agentic AI:** Providing the "operational context infrastructure" for agentic AI ("No AI without PI").
*   **Increase Developer Efficiency:** Expanding the API and MCP surface to enable 1p, 2p, and 3p developers to build AI-driven solutions safely.

## 2. The North Star Metric: TPCM

The primary indicator of market penetration and customer value is **Total Processes Continuously Managed (TPCM)**.

*   **Definition:** The count of customer processes actively managed by the platform. "Actively managed" means frequent operational engagement (via apps, AI agents, automated workflows, or APIs) that steers process cases toward optimal outcomes.
*   **FY27 Goal:** Grow TPCM by **30%**, increasing from **~5,100 to 6,600**.
*   **Significance:** TPCM reflects our shift from periodic "one-off" analysis to a permanent, real-time operational utility that reduces churn and increases stickiness.

## 3. FY27 Core Objectives (L2)

### A. Operational Excellence & Platform Health
*   **Reliability:** Achieve **99.9% service availability**.
*   **Responsiveness:** 100% adherence to CBE (Customer Business Excellence) OLAs.
*   **Efficiency:** $9M in cloud efficiency improvements and reducing average lead time to delivery from 107 to 50 days.

### B. Operational AI Agents ("No AI without PI")
*   **PI Sensors & Triggers:** Enhance process mining to aid in programmatic discovery and triggering of agents along the execution path.
*   **Agentic Orchestration:** Support bidirectional pathways where Celonis feeds context to agents (built on Azure, AWS, etc.) and validates their actions.
*   **Knowledge Lake:** Introduce inefficiency-to-solution playbooks and a "Catalog of Agentic Recipes."

### C. Shared GTM Goals
*   **Supply Chain Flywheel:** Scale repeatable domain impact in Supply Chain (targeting 360 TPCM and a $100M pipeline).
*   **Efficient Builder Experience:** Enable technical users to build custom solutions without domain-specific knowledge (Visual Pipeline Builder, Chain of Thought analysis).
*   **Omnia:** Expand into US Public Sector (FedRAMP/Knox) and European Sovereign (StackIT) realms.

### D. Expanded PI Graph (Digital Twin)
*   **Graph Enrichment:** Move beyond objects and events to natively represent **roles, resources, and systems** in the metamodel.
*   **Data Ubiquity:** Support "Zero Copy" and open standards to "free the process" from source system silos.
*   **Task Mining:** Integrate task-level entities into the PI Graph to provide a granular view of work.

### E. Foundation of ADO
*   **Analyze:** Shift to automated insight and pattern detection using OCPM tools.
*   **Design:** Create a joint persistence layer for process models (BPMN) to enable "Target vs. Actual" adherence monitoring.
*   **Operate:** Launch the **Agentic Composable Enterprise (ACE)** app framework for building AI-driven operational applications.

## 4. Strategic Narrative & Tone

*   **From Insights to Action:** We are not just a dashboarding company; we are an **operational intelligence layer**.
*   **Ground Truth:** The PI Graph is the unique differentiator that provides the context required for AI to be effective in an enterprise setting.
*   **Connected Loop:** The "ADO Loop" is our primary user journey—finding a problem (Analyze), modeling a solution (Design), and executing it (Operate).

## 5. Success Metrics for PRDs

When defining KPIs for a new feature or product, align them with these FY27 benchmarks:

1.  **TPCM Contribution:** Does this feature drive "frequent operational engagement"?
2.  **Builder Productivity:** Does it reduce the lead time for a builder to deploy a solution?
3.  **Process Adherence:** Does it help close the gap between the *designed* process and the *actual* mined process?
4.  **Agent Yield:** If AI-related, does it improve the business outcome of an agent's action?
